Places Must Visit

privithi cafe

i had visited in Dec

Quaint little restuarant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enought to spot the black sign board that says "Privithi Cafe" while you are on the main road. The restaurant has a reasonably good meny at reaosonable prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be by 2 people. Attarcts quite a trendy of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid dissapointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep happening. Hosts the famous "Privithi theater fest", which you must attend if possible.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU

I watched it today with my kids and they loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch infinity war, Civil, Black panther, Age of ultron, Ant-Man and the wasp, before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entiremovie pays homage to most of the other marvel movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginniing of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' action and restore balance to the universe. The movie in legnthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.
